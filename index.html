📚 Back-to-School Campaign — New School Year

Give a Child the Tools to Succeed

A new school year is full of hope and possibility — but for many of the children we support, it also means stress. Without the right supplies, uniforms, and backpacks, these kids start at a disadvantage before they even walk through the school door.

Your donation goes directly to purchasing school essentials for the vulnerable children in our program — backpacks, notebooks, pens, uniforms, and anything else they need to start the year with confidence and dignity.

Our trusted local coordinator in Casablanca personally identifies each child's needs, buys the supplies, and delivers them — with full receipts documented and shared with donors.

Our Mission

Bringing dignity and joy to vulnerable children

We are a group of dedicated professionals with deep ties to Morocco who believe in giving back to the children and communities who need it most.

We have identified children in Morocco who are orphaned, living with disabilities, have absent or disabled parents, or are in extreme poverty. We support them throughout the year with clothing, gifts, and essential items — starting with seasonal celebrations as an example of the joy we strive to bring them every chance we get.

Our long-term vision is to grow our support to cover school expenses, tutoring, medical care, field trips, food, and more. Each campaign is fully transparent: every dirham spent is documented with receipts.

The children have been identified. The person receiving funds in Morocco is trusted. Your donation goes exactly where it should.
